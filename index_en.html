Wedding

Venue

Our wedding takes place in Dwór Korona Karkonoszy, Sosnówka, Poland, on 22nd April 2017.

Wedding Timeline

  • Saturday, April, 22
    • 1230*: shuttle departs from Wroclaw
    • 1500: the shuttle arrives in Sosnowka
    • 1600: wedding ceremony
    • 1700**-0400: party party party
  • Sunday, April, 23
    • 1000 - 1200: after party
    • 1230*: shuttle to Wroclaw departs
    • 1500*: shuttle arrives in Wroclaw

* Those might change, depending on your preferences.

** Polish weddings traditionally begin with Polonez (french Polonaise), our national dance. All guest are warmly encouraged to learn some basic steps!

Directions

Transport

  • Sosnowka is 120 km away from Wroclaw, capital of the region. From Wroclaw, you can get to Sosnowka by car in roughly two hours.
  • We provide a shuttle from Wroclaw to Sosnowka and back, see the timeline above.
  • If the shuttle does not work for you, please let us know. Lots of other guest will drive to the venue, so carpooling is also an option.

Accomodation

On the wedding night

We provide accomodation to all our guest at the venue. The number of one- and two- bed rooms is limited, so we may ask you to share a larger room with other guests, perhaps those whom you like.

Check in: 3pm on Saturday
Check out: 10am on Sunday

Friday and Sunday

It is possible to book a room at the venue a day prior to or after the wedding. It costs around 70 PLN (roughly £15) per night. Please contact us, should you consider this option.
